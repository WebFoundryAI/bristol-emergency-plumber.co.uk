Common Boiler Problems We Repair

Boilers can develop a range of issues, from minor faults to complete breakdowns. Our engineers are trained to diagnose and repair all types of boiler problems:

  • No Heating or Hot Water: The most obvious sign something is wrong. This could indicate various issues from thermostat problems to more serious internal faults.
  • Error Codes: Modern boilers display fault codes when problems occur. Our engineers can interpret these codes and identify the underlying issue.
  • Strange Noises: Banging, whistling, or gurgling sounds often indicate problems such as air in the system, low water pressure, or limescale buildup.
  • Leaking or Dripping: Water leaking from your boiler could be caused by damaged seals, corrosion, or pressure valve issues.
  • Pilot Light Issues: If your pilot light keeps going out, this could indicate a faulty thermocouple or problems with the gas supply.
  • Pressure Problems: Both high and low pressure can affect boiler performance and may indicate leaks or other issues within the system.
  • Frozen Condensate Pipes: During cold weather, condensate pipes can freeze, causing the boiler to shut down as a safety measure.

Gas Safe Registered Engineers

By law, only Gas Safe registered engineers are permitted to work on gas appliances, including boilers. All our engineers who work on gas systems are fully registered with Gas Safe, and we can provide registration details upon request.

This registration means our engineers have demonstrated their competence through rigorous assessment and maintain their qualifications through ongoing training. When you choose our boiler repair service, you can be confident that the work will be carried out safely and to the highest standards.
