Our Plumbing Services in Redland

We provide a full range of plumbing services to homeowners, tenants, and landlords across Redland:

  • Emergency Plumbing Repairs - Plumbing emergencies don't wait for convenient times. That's why our emergency team is available 24/7 to respond to burst pipes, major leaks, flooding, and other urgent situations. We typically arrive within 30-60 minutes and carry comprehensive parts to fix most problems on the spot.
  • Boiler Repair and Servicing - Our Gas Safe registered engineers are experienced with all boiler types, from modern combi boilers to traditional system boilers with hot water cylinders. We offer emergency repairs, annual servicing to maintain efficiency and warranty compliance, and professional advice on boiler replacement when necessary.
  • Blocked Drains and Drainage - Redland's Victorian drainage infrastructure can be prone to blockages and root ingress. We provide professional drain unblocking using high-pressure jetting, CCTV drain surveys to identify problems, and drainage repairs including relining and excavation when required.
  • Bathroom Plumbing - From replacing a dripping tap to complete bathroom installations, we handle all aspects of bathroom plumbing. We can work sensitively with period features such as original roll-top baths or install contemporary suites in keeping with your property's character.
  • Central Heating Installation and Repair - We install, repair, and maintain central heating systems of all types. Services include radiator installation and repair, system balancing, powerflush treatments to restore efficiency, and complete heating system upgrades for older properties.
  • Leak Detection Services - Hidden leaks can cause significant damage to Redland's period properties. We use specialist acoustic and thermal detection equipment to locate leaks with minimal disruption, protecting original floorboards, plasterwork, and other features.
  • Pipework Upgrades - Many Redland properties still have sections of original lead or iron pipework. We can assess your existing plumbing and carry out partial or complete repiping to improve water quality, pressure, and reliability.
