Our Plumbing Services in Kingsdown

We deliver comprehensive plumbing services for all property types across Kingsdown:

  • Emergency Plumbing Repairs - Plumbing emergencies require immediate attention, and our team is ready to respond at any hour. We deal with burst pipes, major leaks, flooding, boiler breakdowns, blocked toilets, and all other urgent situations. Our emergency plumbers typically reach Kingsdown addresses within 30-60 minutes and carry comprehensive parts to resolve most problems on the first visit.
  • Boiler Repair and Servicing - Kingsdown's elevated position and older housing stock mean heating systems work hard during Bristol's cold months. Our Gas Safe registered engineers repair all boiler makes and models, provide annual servicing to maintain efficiency and safety, and offer honest advice about replacement when boilers reach the end of their useful life.
  • Blocked Drains and Drainage - The steep terrain in Kingsdown can create particular drainage challenges. We provide professional drain unblocking using high-pressure jetting, CCTV surveys to investigate persistent problems, and drainage repairs from minor fixes to major reconstruction work on older systems.

We install new radiators, repair faulty components, balance systems to eliminate cold spots, perform powerflush treatments to improve efficiency, and install complete new heating systems when required.
  • Landlord and HMO Services - Many Kingsdown properties are let to students and professionals. We work with landlords and letting agents to provide gas safety certificates, emergency callout cover, planned maintenance programmes, and responsive repairs that keep tenants satisfied and properties compliant with regulations.
