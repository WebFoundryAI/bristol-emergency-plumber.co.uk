What Constitutes a Plumbing Emergency?

Not every plumbing issue requires immediate attention, but some situations demand urgent professional intervention. Our emergency service is designed to address:

  • Burst Pipes: Perhaps the most common plumbing emergency, a burst pipe can release hundreds of litres of water into your property within minutes. Whether caused by freezing temperatures, corrosion, or physical damage, burst pipes require immediate professional attention.
  • Severe Flooding: When water is actively flooding your property from any source, immediate action is essential to minimise damage to your home and belongings.
  • Gas Leaks: If you smell gas in your property, this constitutes a serious emergency. Our Gas Safe registered engineers can safely identify and repair gas leaks.
  • Complete Loss of Water: Losing your water supply entirely can be both inconvenient and concerning, potentially indicating a serious problem with your plumbing system.
  • Sewage Backups: Raw sewage backing up into your property presents serious health hazards and requires immediate professional intervention.
  • Boiler Failures in Cold Weather: During winter months, a complete heating failure can be dangerous, particularly for elderly residents or those with health conditions.

Our Emergency Response Process
